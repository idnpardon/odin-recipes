Manty Recipe

Manti is a slow-cooking dish consisting of large steamed dumplings filled with meat (or pumpkin or cheese), spices, and onions, encased in thin dough and steamed in a pre-cooked pot called a manti-maker. They are distinguished by the juiciness of the filling, which often includes pieces of lard or finely chopped meat.

Ingredients

  • dough
  • meat
  • onion
  • potato

Steps

  1. make the dough
  2. make minced meat
  3. cut the potatoes
  4. cut the onions
  5. make the filling
  6. make the manty
  7. steam the manty
