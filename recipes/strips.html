Strips Recipe

Chicken strips are crispy fried pieces of chicken breast coated in a flavorful breading. They’re golden on the outside, tender inside, and perfect for dipping in sauces. A quick and easy recipe for lunch, dinner, or a party snack.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast — 500 g
  • Flour — 100 g (about ¾ cup)
  • Carrots — 3–4 pcs
  • Eggs — 2 pcs
  • Bread crumbs — 150 g
  • Salt — 1 tsp
  • Black pepper — ½ tsp
  • Paprika — 1 tsp
  • Garlic powder — ½ tsp
  • Vegetable oil — for frying

Steps

  1. Cut chicken breasts into long, even strips. Pat dry with paper towels.
  2. In a bowl, mix salt, black pepper, paprika, and garlic powder. Rub the mixture onto the chicken strips.
  3. Dip each strip in flour, then in egg, and finally coat with bread crumbs. Press lightly so the coating sticks well.
  4. Pour enough oil into a frying pan to cover the bottom (about 1–2 cm). Heat on medium-high until hot.
  5. Fry the chicken strips in batches for about 3–4 minutes per side until golden and crispy. Don’t overcrowd the pan.
  6. Place the cooked strips on a plate lined with paper towels to absorb excess oil.
